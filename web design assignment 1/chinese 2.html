Apple and Date Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 red apples
  • 1/2 cup pitted dates
  • 1/4 cup flaked coconut, toasted

    • Dressing

  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup salad oil
  • 4 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 2 teaspoons dark rum

Method

Cut apples into thin wedges. Cut dates into thin strips. Combine apples, dates, coconut and dressing in bowl; mix gently.
Dressing: Combine all ingredients in screw-top jar; shake well.
Serves 6.
